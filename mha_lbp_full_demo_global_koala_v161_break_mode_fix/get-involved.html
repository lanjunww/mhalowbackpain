Get involved

Help make back pain support more practical, personal and accessible

People do not only need information. They need encouragement, community, and simple ways to stay engaged. This page shows how users, volunteers and supporters can contribute to MHA’s low back pain support ecosystem.

Why involvement matters

User feedback, lived experience and community support help improve the quality and relevance of personalised back pain tools.
