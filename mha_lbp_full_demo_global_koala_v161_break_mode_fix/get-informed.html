Safety note

This demo provides general information and self-management support. Users should seek professional advice if pain is severe, worsening, linked with injury, or accompanied by concerning symptoms such as new weakness, numbness, bladder or bowel changes, fever, unexplained weight loss, or feeling seriously unwell. High-risk users should be guided toward support rather than only receiving activity nudges.

Common questions

Short answers keep the page useful without becoming a dense article library.

Should I rest completely when my back hurts?

For many people, avoiding long periods of complete rest and gently returning to normal activity can help. The plan focuses on small, safe actions rather than intense exercise.

Why does the tool ask about sleep, stress and sitting?

Low back pain can be affected by daily patterns, not only by one physical cause. These questions help the system identify which support is most relevant.

Is Koala giving medical advice?

No. Koala provides gentle behaviour nudges, such as changing position or taking a short break. It does not diagnose or replace professional care.
