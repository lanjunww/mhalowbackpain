MacBook desktop demo

MHA Low Back Pain site in Chrome and Safari

Open either browser from the dock. The website launches on the homepage. Even if the browser is minimised, Koala remains in the top menu bar and sitting reminders can still appear.

How to demo it Open Chrome or Safari → go to Reminders or the final follow-up question → turn on Koala sitting reminders. Then minimise the browser. The top Koala keeps running.
